About Robot Overlord

We work closely with partners to devlop world class products. We are selective about finding a good fit, yadda yadda.

Brooklyn Zelenka and Jenn Cooper are the founding partners at Robot Overlord Software, a functionally-focused web and mobile app agency based out of Vancouver, Canada. They have written many production Phoenix apps, and released several Elixir libraries including Witchcraft (“Scalaz for Elixir”) and Exceptional (the second most downloaded error handling library on Hex).

Both have long histories teaching and mentoring in grass roots settings and with non-profits such as Ladies Learning Code, covering languages such as Elixir, Clojure, Racket, Ruby, Javascript, and Haskell. They are also the founders and organizers of Code & Coffee, Vancouver Erlang & Elixir, and the Vancouver Functional Programmers meetups. Brooklyn is also the recipient of the 2017 Women in Tech Mentorship Award for her efforts bringing functional programming to the Vancouver tech community.
